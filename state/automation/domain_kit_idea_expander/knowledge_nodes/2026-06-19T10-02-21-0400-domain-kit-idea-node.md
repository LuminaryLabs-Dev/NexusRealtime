## Root Lesson
- id: domain-kit-idea-root-2026-06-19-1002
- statement: The next useful expansion layer is state signal contracts: descriptor invalidation, action repeat policy, small state-machine transition receipts, coordinate-axis policy, derived-state boundaries, and proof rows that explain why state changed, repeated, rebuilt, or stayed source-owned.
- why it matters: Fresh neighboring bug evidence shows reusable services can miss meaningful route changes, repeat one-shot actions from held booleans, start or transition terminal state ambiguously, ignore common `{ x, z }` coordinates, or mutate source camera targets during safety adjustment.
