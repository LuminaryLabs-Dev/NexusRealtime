## Root Lesson
- id: domain-kit-idea-root-2026-06-19-0701
- statement: The next useful expansion layer is receipt integrity: stable action/result ids, transition-only event emission, duplicate claim policy, generated request identity, and proof rows that keep telemetry/replay attribution deterministic.
- why it matters: Fresh neighboring bug evidence shows service APIs can keep state mostly correct while corrupting or repeating emitted receipts. DSK promotion needs receipt identity and idempotent event surfaces in addition to accepted mutation, stable id allocation, and spatial/mobility invariants.
