## Root Lesson
- id: deep-bug-root-2026-06-19-placement-interaction-driver-camera
- statement: Route-aware placement, base interaction, puzzle/combat transitions, validation steering, and camera occlusion need invalidation, edge semantics, terminal-state, coordinate, and derived-state fixtures before they can be trusted as reusable DSK proof surfaces.
- why it matters: Current APIs can keep stale placement descriptors after route edits, repeat one-shot receipts from held booleans, start combat in contradictory terminal state, ignore `z`-axis steering, and mutate camera look targets during occlusion adjustment.
